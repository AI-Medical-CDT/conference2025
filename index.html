About

The UKRI CDT in AI for Medical Diagnosis and Care is training over 50 graduates to become leaders in the application of artificial intelligence to healthcare. With a focus on research excellence, collaboration, and innovation, the Centre is committed to producing highly skilled professionals who can harness AI to develop secure, privacy-compliant solutions for medical diagnosis and care. By fostering industry partnerships and international collaborations, the Centre aims to position the UK as a global leader in health-related AI research and technology transfer.

Our annual conference brings together the CDT’s students, academics, and industry partners, offering a dynamic platform to explore and discuss the latest research from the CDT. Students will share updates on their progress, while our speakers will offer new perspectives to enrich the community. We hope that the event will provide an opportunity for our diverse network of researchers and healthcare professionals to reconnect and seek further opportunities for collaboration.
