Rashik Parmar is a renowned digital leader with over four decades of experience driving responsible and impactful innovation. Formerly Group CEO of BCS and an IBM Fellow, he has shaped technical strategy and championed Responsible Computing. He serves in advisory roles with UKRI, The Open University, Leeds University Business School, and Digit Lab. He is also a founding director of Rangoli Radio, and author of Sunday Sprinkles, a blog exploring personal happiness through thoughtful reflection.
